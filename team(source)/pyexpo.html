ABOUT BLE BEACONS

A BLE sensor is a sensor that uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology to transmit data wirelessly. BLE is a low-power version of Bluetooth, which makes it ideal for use in battery-powered devices. BLE sensors can be used to measure a variety of physical and environmental parameters, such as temperature, humidity, light, motion, and pressure
